Project Name: Connect4

Project Description

The Connect 4 AUI (Artificial Intelligence Integration) project is an innovative computer project that aims to enhance the classic game of Connect 4 by incorporating advanced artificial intelligence capabilities. Connect 4 AUI leverages cutting-edge machine learning algorithms and AI techniques to create a challenging and dynamic gaming experience for players.

The project focuses on developing an intelligent and adaptive AI system that can analyze the current game state, anticipate player moves, and strategically make its own moves to compete at a high level. The AI is designed to learn and improve over time, providing players with increasingly formidable opponents and ensuring an engaging and competitive gameplay experience.

Connect 4 AUI also introduces a user-friendly interface that allows players to interact seamlessly with the AI. The graphical user interface (GUI) is designed for ease of use, featuring intuitive controls and visually appealing elements. Players can choose from various difficulty levels, ranging from beginner to expert, providing a customized experience for users of all skill levels.
